About Hidden Horizons

Hidden Horizons is a student-led project by DBRAIT to promote respectful, low-impact travel across the Andaman & Nicobar Islands.

  • Highlight hidden nature spots, culture, and local foods
  • Connect travelers with one trusted local guide per location
  • Encourage eco-friendly and community-first tourism
